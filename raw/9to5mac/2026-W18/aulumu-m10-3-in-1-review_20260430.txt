Aulumu M10: 3-in-1 iPhone MagSafe battery also recharges Apple Watch and AirPods

iPhone MagSafe battery packs are fantastic for extending device usage between charging sessions. While most solutions only work with compatible iPhones, the Aulumu M10 Multi-Charging Dual-Mag Power Bank is a high-capacity MagSafe battery pack that does the job of three distinct chargers.

Aulumu M10 combines iPhone MagSafe with an Apple Watch charger and USB-C

The Aulumu M10 Multi-Charging Dual-Mag Power Bank is far from the sleekest MagSafe battery pack. Instead, it delivers 3-in-1 charging functionality in a futuristic design that stands out. Aulumu uses a distinct style across its products that is meant to look technical, industrial, and far from boring.

This 10,000mAh capacity portable battery pack doesn’t try to be the slimmest solution. Rather, it maximizes utility by sharing three built-in charging methods.

MagSafe that holds your iPhone, not the other way around

First, there’s the MagSafe side.

This lets the Aulumu M10 attach magnetically to the back of your iPhone for wireless charging during use. Again, not a super pocket-friendly battery pack, but that’s not the goal.

Instead, the battery pack is substantial enough that you really hold it as your iPhone magnetically attaches, not vice versa.

Built-in Apple Watch charger pulls double duty

Flip it over and the Aulumu M10 has a different magnetic charging solution. The smaller charging puck lets you attach your Apple Watch or compatible AirPods charging case.

Having this solution as part of a MagSafe battery pack is really practical. A compatible AirPods charging case can also use MagSafe, but the Apple Watch puck lets you power up your iPhone and AirPods charging case at the same time.

Meanwhile, the Apple Watch can only be powered up from one method, and that be included with a MagSafe battery pack is ideal.

USB-C for the fastest power delivery in a pinch

Lastly, there’s the third charging output in this 3-in-1 device: UBS-C.

The Aulumu M10 includes a built-in USB-C cable for charging over the wire. It neatly tucks into the battery pack so you don’t have a dangling cord, and it’s not removable so you’ll always have it with you.

The USB-C cable is great if you want the fastest charging solution for your iPhone in a pinch. You can also use it to deliver power to an iPad (common in my family) or a MacBook.

Again, having all three charging solutions in one compact battery pack is wonderful.

The Aulumu M10 is all about utility over being super slim, and it delivers all this functionality in a cool and futuristic form.

Key specs for Aulumu M10 Multi-Charging Dual-Mag Power Bank:

Cell Capacity: 10000mAh

Weight: 248g

Size: 270mm x 75mm (Cable Included), Cable: 160mm

USB-C Cable / Port Input: 5V⎓3A, 9V⎓3A, 12V⎓2.91A, 15V⎓2.33A, 20V⎓1.75A (35W Max)

USB-C Cable / Port Output: 5V⎓3A, 9V⎓3A, 12V⎓2.91A, 15V⎓2.33A, 20V⎓1.75A (35W Max)

Apple Watch Charging Output: 2.5W Max

MagSafe Charging Output: 5W / 7.5W / 10W / 15W

Multi-Output Power: 15W Max

Aulumu M10 Multi-Charging Dual-Mag Power Bank is available for $89.98 from aulumu.com and sometimes Amazon.
